User Scenario Mapping

Scenario

Josh is a college student who has very little money and barely affords his classes at school. He has had the same pair of shoes for the last 3 years. One day, Josh slipped down the stairs and tore right through one of his shoes. He found Converse, a store that sells high quality shoes for a affordable price. He decides to order himself a pair of new shoes that will last him a long long time.

Actor

Josh

Motivator

Needing a new pair of shoes

Intention

Josh wants an affordable pair of shoes that will last a long time.

Action

Josh starts looking online to find a low priced shoe that is high quality in durability. Josh comes across Converse, a shoe store.

Resolution

He makes an online purchase for a new pair of Converse shoes.
